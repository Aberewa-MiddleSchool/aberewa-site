The Seven-for-Seven Classroom Commitment

Each instructional group of up to seven students will have one educator responsible for instruction, progress monitoring, and collaboration, plus one paraprofessional supporting access, participation, regulation, communication, and classroom routines.

This model is intended for students with specific learning disabilities, autism, and other disability-related needs within the school’s Year One support capacity. Placement will be based on individual needs rather than a label alone.

A Cohort Model Designed for Continuity

Year One

The school will enroll seventh-grade students only. Every Aberewa student begins the program in seventh grade.

Year Two

The inaugural cohort advances to eighth grade while a new seventh-grade cohort enters. There will be no direct eighth-grade entry.

This sequence supports relationships, shared routines, and individualized planning before the transition to eighth grade. Future total enrollment will depend on building capacity, staffing, funding, and the school’s ability to maintain its small-group commitment.
