Student Support

Student support and readiness

Seven-for-Seven

Each instructional group of up to seven students is planned to include one educator and one paraprofessional.

Complete Year One plan

Opening-Year Vision

Seventh grade begins the journey.

Year One is planned for seventh-grade entry only, with phased enrollment of up to 70 students. The inaugural cohort advances to eighth grade in Year Two while a new seventh-grade cohort may enter. No student will enter directly into eighth grade.

7students per group
1 + 1educator and paraprofessional
Up to 70Year One planning capacity

Opening enrollment may begin with 28 students and expand to 49 and then 70 only as facilities, approvals, qualified staffing, funding, and student-support systems are confirmed.
