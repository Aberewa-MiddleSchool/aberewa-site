Long-term vision

What we are working toward

People

Teachers, special educators, related-service professionals, classroom support staff, administrators, and operations personnel.

Learning

Accessible curriculum, assistive technology, sensory supports, educational materials, arts, music, and culturally rooted programming.

Facilities

A safe, accessible campus capable of supporting an eventual community of approximately 500–700 neurodivergent students.

Planning

Legal, financial, architectural, regulatory, research, community-engagement, and organizational work required before opening.
