Long-term vision

What we are working toward

People

Teachers, special educators, related-service professionals, classroom support staff, administrators, and operations personnel.

Learning

Accessible curriculum, assistive technology, sensory supports, educational materials, arts, music, and culturally rooted programming.

Facilities

A safe, accessible opening campus for a phased Year One program of up to 70 students, with a long-term aspiration to expand only as funding, approvals, staffing, services, and demonstrated results allow.

Planning

Legal, financial, architectural, regulatory, research, community-engagement, and organizational work required before opening.

Accountability and updates

Our public Roadmap to Opening identifies the legal, financial, facility, staffing, safety, and educational milestones that must be completed before the school opens.
